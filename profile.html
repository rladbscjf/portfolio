ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Yunchul Kim and I am currently a student at STI College - Global City studying Information Technology. I've loved computers ever since I was a kid. It all started when my Aunt used to take me to her office filled with all sorts of computers and technology. Since then, I've become fascinated with how computers work internally.

I've also studied Architecture for 2 years before deciding that I really wanted to pursue a career in technology. I never once regretted taking Architecture because it is there where I met such amazing people that I now call my friends and how I found a passion for designing that I now use when creating webpages like this.
