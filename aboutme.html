My philosophy towards work is to challenge myself continuously, to be creative in everything I do for client or a company. Learning new things, taking new opportunities and working with amazing and creative people are one of the ways I find motivation. I always strive to perform well in something especially if it is an important task because I always see it as a reflection of who I am as a person. All in all, if I can enjoy what I am doing, I can work harder.

IT is an industry where we should never stop learning new things and it can sometimes burn you out. If you have the chance, relax, try developing new skills, learn a new hobby or look for something that makes you passionate about. These are the things I usually do so that I get through tough times, either from school, work, or even life.
